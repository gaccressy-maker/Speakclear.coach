The Insight Behind SpeakClear

If you can't say it clearly — people stop listening.

In Malaysia, Singapore and across Asia, I kept meeting the same person. Talented. Hardworking. Deserving of the next step up. But in meetings, with clients, with senior colleagues — something got in the way. Not knowledge. Not ability. Just the moment between thinking and speaking — where confidence drops, pronunciation lets you down, and the professional you actually are doesn't come through.

Pronunciation is not just about speaking. It is the key to understanding and being understood. When you learn to say words naturally, you begin to hear them more clearly too. That is where real communication confidence begins.

With over 30 years working as a professional across demanding industries, I have seen first-hand how much communication under pressure affects confidence, performance and opportunity — for people at every stage of their career.

SpeakClear was created specifically for professionals whose English should reflect how capable they actually are.

Our Approach

More than traditional English teaching

SpeakClear is not a grammar course. It is not a classroom. It is a warm, relaxed coaching space where real conversation is practised in real situations — at your own pace, without the fear of getting it wrong.

What many professionals experience before coming to SpeakClear:

  • Knowing what to say but struggling to say it naturally in the moment
  • Confidence dropping in fast-moving meetings and presentations
  • Translating in your head before you can speak — losing your place
  • Pronunciation making it harder to be taken seriously than you deserve
